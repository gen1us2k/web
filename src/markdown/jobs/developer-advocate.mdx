## Developer Advocate

<About />

You will work with an international community of developers, help solve their
problems and grow the Ory movement.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Remote (Timezone CET ± 3H) / Munich, Germany
- **Components**: Ory Open Source, Slack, Socials

### What You'll Do

- Collaborate with an international, remote-first, and distributed team of open
  source enthusiasts.
- Create content for the Ory Community that engages, educates and inspires
  developers (e.g. blog posts, tutorials, videos, presentations, live streams,
  sample code, demo apps courses, demo apps).
- Propose and create content on developer topics that you’re passionate about
  (with no limits as long as it excites you and would excite other developers).
- Attend and speak at events like conferences, meetups or hackathons (online or
  in-person, if Covid regulations permit).
- Engage with our community both in-person and online (e.g. via GitHub and
  Slack) to understand user needs and generate insights on how to improve Ory’s
  developer experience.
- Collaborate with Ory product managers, engineers, and marketers to improve
  developer experience, dream up great content ideas, and coordinate events
  (e.g. the Ory Summit 2022).

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Interest in a career in developer relations.
- Experience as a developer in a related WebDev/API/SaaS technology field.
- Experience in creating developer content for audiences of varying expertise,
  using one of the following languages: JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, .NET/C#,
  Ruby, PHP, or Go.
- Experience with publishing content, either professionally or personally.
- Social and public speaking skills (e.g. at meetups or developer conferences).
- Facility with words, writing, and general communication, plus an ability to
  explain technical concepts to audiences of varying technical expertise.
- Empathy and enthusiasm for community building.

_We encourage you to apply even if you do not meet all the requirements - there
is always an opportunity to learn on the job._

## Digital Campaign Manager

<About />

The Digital Campaign Manager is responsible for developing strategic plans for
communicating Ory’s brand message on an international level to expand reach
among prospects and customers across multiple channels. This includes
development and execution of end-to-end, integrated B2B demand generation
programs as well as building and maintaining a campaign calendar to plan and
optimize data-driven demand generation campaigns.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: Google Suite, Hubspot, GitHub

### What You'll Do

- Conceptualize, execute and optimize data-driven demand generation campaigns to
  expand Ory’s reach among prospects and customers across multiple channels.
- Develop integrated marketing campaigns aligning with internal teams, develop
  audience segments and plan top-funnel virtual events.
- Analyze full-funnel campaign performance on an ongoing basis to continuously
  optimize results
- Collaborate to create impactful marketing content for our audience of
  developers and IT leaders.
- Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to ensure proper
  messaging, timing, and execution of multi-channel campaigns.
- Apply an experimentation mindset to marketing campaigns to test, learn and
  optimize; continuously conduct A/B testing across all channels.
- Build prescriptive persona-based buyer journeys that drive campaign strategy.
- Grow and shape Ory’s presence as a global brand with open source roots.
- Explore new channels for reaching relevant audiences for Ory Cloud.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- 3+ years experience in developing digital marketing campaigns.
- A deep understanding of customer and channel buying cycles.
- Strong project management, organizational, and communication skills.
- Strong writing skills and ability to explain technical concepts to a wide
  audience with varying levels of technical expertise.
- Experience with A/B testing, experiment design, campaign analysis (attribution
  reporting is a plus).
- A firm grasp of industry standards and emerging trends in digital marketing.
- An ability to self-manage and collaborate across teams.
- An ability to generate marketing insights and report key findings.
- A willingness to try out creative new ideas and iterate on them.
- Experience with CRM software (ideally Hubspot).
- Experience working in a B2B or Cloud software marketing team.
- Fluency in written and oral English.

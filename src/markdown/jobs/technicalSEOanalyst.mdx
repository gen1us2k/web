## Technical SEO Analyst

<About />

Ory is the largest open-source authentication and authorization (login,
registration, MFA, permissions, OAuth2) ecosystem and community in the world. We
develop open-source software and use it to build a scalable, easy-to-use, and
secure cloud platform. You will provide organizational support to Ory leadership
to ensure smooth operations and communications. Together with the CEO, you will
provide a core leadership function that collaborates with UX design,
engineering, finance, marketing, and data analytics.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: DeepCrawl, Screaming Frog, and SEO Rada

### What You'll Do

- Lead technical SEO efforts in collaboration with web development and product teams
- Assist on general SEO projects such as keyword research, content planning, and other internal SEO lead work
- Monitor Ory‘s web presence – finding and resolving technical or content issues that arise
- Help us determine internal web content accessibility standards and understand how they impact SEO
- Communicate SEO best practices to the rest of the marketing team, and make sure they’re being followed. 
- Devise a best practice documentation scheme to communicate easi with engineering teams
- Work with growth marketing people to foresee impact to SEO during A/B testing experiments. 
- Communicate SEO roadmaps to relevant members of company leadership
- Stay in the know on industry best practices and innovations in technical SEO
- Assist on wider SEO objectives such as site migrations, page deprecations, and more


### We’re looking for someone who has

- 4 + years of experience working in technical SEO with multiple CMS platforms.
- Understanding of core SEO principles
- Ability to collaborate across departments, including engineering
- A technical understanding of HTML and JavaScript frameworks and how to optimize them. (Additional frameworks and other programming languages are beneficial.)
- Experience working with different crawlers to identify technical issues (i.e. DeepCrawl, Screaming Frog, and SEO Radar)
- Ability to communicate technical concepts to a wide audience with varying levels of technical expertise 
- Knowledge of Core Web Vitals and their impact on SEO
- Ability to develop and understand page schema
- Understanding of sitemaps and hreflang
- Understanding of how SEO works in relation to Google crawls

## Solutions Engineer

<About />
<SalesGeneric />

### Overview

- **Position type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany / Remote (Timezone CET ± 3H)
- **Components**: Ory Cloud Console, Ory Open Source

### What You'll Do

- Talk to customers, write code, debug and solve problems.
- Become an expert at using the Ory Cloud and Ory Open Source products.
- Work with the Product Management, Engineering, and Sales teams to advocate for
  our customers.
- Collaborate with the Ory Open Source community to build and expand the Ory
  Open Source ecosystem.
- Help set the tone for future hires and develop the sales and solutions team.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- A Bachelor’s Degree in an analytical or technical subject field or equivalent
  experience.
- Experience at a SaaS provider or working with open source products with a
  commercial Cloud offering.
- Excellent scripting knowledge (e.g. BASH, Python, Ruby, Perl).
- Familiarity with at least one open source database (MySQL, PostgreSQL).
- Strong attention to detail, communication, and writing skills.
- Fluent written and spoken English skills (and any other languages are a plus).
- Ideally, prior experience in a communications role.

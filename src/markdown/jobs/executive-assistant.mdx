## Executive Assistant

<About />

You will provide organizational support to Ory leadership to ensure smooth
operations and communications. Together with the CEO, you will provide a core
leadership function that collaborates with UX design, engineering, finance,
marketing, and data analytics.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: Google Suite, Slack, Github

### What You'll Do

- Manage the executive calender, scheduling appointments, company meetings,and
  travel itineraries.
- Coordinate communications within Germany and internationally.
- Organize company events, e.g. the monthly All-Hands Meeting.
- Collaborate with the product management, engineering, sales and marketing
  teams to facilitate well-rounded decision making with a bias for action and
  calculated risks.
- Collaborate with the sales and marketing teams to facilitate rapid time to
  value for our customers and prospective customers.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Excellent oral and written communication skills in English and German.
- Ability to write precise communications on behalf of the CEO.
- A high level of organizational skills, attention to detail, and ability to
  create and meet efficient deadlines.
- Curiosity, creativity and an eagerness to develop new competencies as new
  challenges arise.
- Experience in collaborative management with design, engineering, data and
  analytics, marketing, and operations.
- 2+ years of experience in an assistance role, ideally at a software company
  and/or startup.
- Talent for understanding and explaining customer problems.
- Technical fluency with G-Suite tools, Slack.
- Basic accounting/finance management skills.
- Ability to maintain discretion, confidentiality, and professionalism.

## Digital Growth Manager

<About />

The Digital Growth Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and managing
the digital demand strategy and Ory marketing campaigns across paid search, paid
social and sponsored publisher channels. This role will drive digital campaign
execution across integrated marketing objectives, and will develop and manage
demand execution to drive product adoption and meet pipeline goals from both
inbound and outbound campaigns.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: HubSpot, Google Analytics

### What You'll Do

- Build customer-focused programs and digital campaigns to help drive
  data-driven strategic campaign recommendations.
- Create and expand digital channels to engage prospects and customers to
  quickly scale acquisition strategy and drive retention.
- Deliver insights to drive campaign optimization and identify personalized
  conversion journeys across different groups of users.
- Analyze and report data across all existing channels to monitor marketing site
  growth.
- Manage paid demand generation strategy including budgets (project plans) to
  assist in building the sales pipeline by driving high-quality leads.
- Build and adjust marketing operations infrastructure that incorporates
  performance measurements and analytics.
- Collaborate with team members (and perhaps an external agency) to execute on
  SEO priorities to increase organic traffic to Ory and associated
  websites.
- Collaborate with other departments (HR, Sales, Engineering) to maximize yield
  from shared channels and programs.
- Drive innovation and experimentation to improve demand creation & conversion.
- Test new lead generation approaches and share key insights.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Experience with modern digital growth strategies and technical/industry
  experience focused on SEO, SEM, paid social and/or web conversion rate
  optimization to improve lead generation, sales pipeline, and revenue.
- A deep understanding of performance data (e.g. CAC, LTV, conversion rate,
  return on ad spend, customer retention rates and churn).
- Experience in successfully building and improving paid demand generation
  campaigns for product-focused SaaS enterprises in the B2B market.
- Strong analytical skills with an ability to compile and analyze campaign data
  to inform strategic decisions at any part of the funnel.
- Experience working closely with technical product marketing managers to
  amplify key content and features.
- Experience leading cross-departmental projects and collaborating with web
  developers to drive meaningful impact – plus enthusiasm for such interdisciplinary collaboration. 
- An entrepreneurial mindset and hands-on approach to solving problems. 
- Attention to detail.
- Fluency in written and oral English.

## Developer Advocate Intern

<About />

As Developer Relations Intern, you will work with an international community of
developers, help solve their problems and grow the Ory movement. It is an
opportunity to explore a highly social, intellectually stimulating career path
at a young, fast-paced startup with a focus on open source software.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Student Worker, Part Time
- **Location**: Remote (Timezone CET) / Munich, Germany
- **Components**: Ory Open Source, Slack, Socials

### What You'll Do

- Collaborate with an international, remote-first, and distributed team of open
  source enthusiasts.
- Create content for the Ory Community that engages, educates, and inspires
  developers (e.g. blog posts, tutorials, videos, presentations, live streams,
  sample code, demo apps courses, demo apps).
- Propose and create content on developer topics that you’re passionate about
  (no limitations as long as it excites you and would excite other developers!).
- Attend and speak at events like conferences, meetups, or hackathons (online or
  in-person, if Covid regulations permit).
- Engage with our community both in-person and online (e.g. via GitHub, Slack,
  and other channels) to understand user needs and generate insights on how to
  improve Ory’s developer experience.
- Collaborate with Ory product managers, engineers, marketers to improve
  developer experience, dream up great content ideas, and coordinate events
  (e.g. the Ory Summit 2022).

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Basic experience with JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS and programming
  fundamentals.
- Interest in working in open source developer relations.
- Enthusiasm for creating developer content for audiences of varying expertise,
  using modern web development frameworks and languages.
- Excellent communication (writing, speaking and social) skills.
- Enthusiasm, empathy and passion when explaining technical concepts to members
  of the Ory community.

_We encourage you to apply even if you do not meet all these requirements -
there is always an opportunity to learn on the job!_
